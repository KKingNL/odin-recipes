Lasagna

How to make delicious lasagna

The best way to make lasagna is always with fresh ingredients and when possible from scratch.

The trick is though to always make it with love.

Ingredients:
  • Minced meat
  • Tomatoes
  • Carrot
  • Salt and pepper
  1. Cook the meat
  2. Add the tomatoes
  3. Add the rest
  4. Toss it in the oven
