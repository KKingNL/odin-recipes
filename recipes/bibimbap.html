Bibimbap

How to make delicious Bibimbap

The best way to make bibimbap is always with fresh ingredients.

This dish is one of my personal secrets.

Ingredients:
  • Minced meat
  • Rice
  • Carrots jullienne
  • Egg
  • Sweet bellpepper
  • Cucumber
  • Sesame seeds
  • Bulgogi marinade
  • Lime
  1. Marinade the meat for 3 hours
  2. Cook the meat
  3. Cut the bell pepper and cucumber into cubes
  4. Cook the rice
  5. Toast the sesame seeds and then mix with the carrots and lime juice
  6. Bake an egg sunny side up and leave the yolk a bit runny
  7. plate the rice and top with the meat, vegetables and the egg
  8. add some Ssamjjang on the side
  9. Kamsamnida :)
